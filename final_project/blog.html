Thespian Society

August, 2019 Mari

A glorified acting club, the Hamden Hall thespians present multiple performances throughout a school year, launching the season with a haunted house fundraiser. For the rest of the year, students have plenty of opportunities to channel their inner shakespeare and study plays, musicals, one-acts, and monologues.

Students can also work direct their own shows, stage manage or participate as a crew member.

Hamden Hall Choir

August 7, 2019 by Mari

Hamden Hall Choirs is one of the states leading high school choral programs, with one chamber choir, and two honor choirs. Students are asked to challenge themselves with music from various periods and their ability to interprate music.

  • Students participate in two anual concerts.
  • Regional and state music festivals
  • A trip to six flags!
